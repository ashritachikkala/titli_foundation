The Problem
/taboo/
72% women don't have access to hygienic period products and due to this girls drop out of school due to taboo, and Titli aims to tackle this problem.
Groundwork Gallery
Vision
Make sustainable menstruation products accessible to all

We here at Titli envisage a world where menstruation is no longer a taboo and sustainable menstrual products are easily accessible to all and Titli works tirelssly for the same.

Mission
Break the Taboo

Create awareness around menstrual hygiene, break generational taboos around it and promote sustainable practices through a change in menstrual habits.
