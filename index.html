23 Million menstruators drop out of school annually in India due to period poverty.
Period Poverty
is the lack of access to menstrual products and awareness about menstruation.
Taboo and Stigma
around menstruation plays a huge role in contributing to period poverty.
We aim to
fight
period poverty.
Titli is a registered youth led NGO established on 20th May, 2020, actively working towards helping menstruators switch to a sustainable menstrual lifestyle and break the taboo around menstruation.

Titli donates eco-friendly products like the menstrual cup, cloth and compostable pads to underprivileged menstruators to attain this goal.
